It's been said that you only track the things you want to change. Countism is an app that will help you track everything that matters to you with timeseries data, graphs, averages, and more so you can see how you're doing and improving over time.

Track things like how many glasses of water you drink a day, how many times you go to the gym or go for a jog, how many times you swear, or whatever.

Track what matters. Change your life.
